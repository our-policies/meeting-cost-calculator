Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Meeting Cost Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your email address via Google Firebase Authentication to create and manage your account.
  • Meeting Data: We store data related to your meetings, such as duration, calculated cost, number of participants, and timestamps. This data is stored securely in Google Cloud Firestore linked to your user ID.
  • Subscription Status: We process data regarding your "Pro" status verification through Lemon Squeezy. We do not store your full credit card information; payments are processed entirely by Lemon Squeezy.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the extension (calculating and saving meeting costs).
  • To synchronize your meeting history across different devices.
  • To verify your subscription status for premium features.
  • To maintain and improve the performance of our service.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our extension:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, database storage, and hosting. (Read Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Lemon Squeezy: Used for processing payments and subscription management. (Read Lemon Squeezy's Privacy Policy)

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To request deletion, please contact us at the email provided below.
